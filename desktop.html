About Us

Nova is a distinguished brand originating from the scenic state of Nagaland, specializing in the manufacturing of high-quality drinks and juices. Embracing the rich natural bounty of the region, Nova is dedicated to delivering refreshing and flavorful beverages that embody the essence of Nagaland's unique produce. Nova aims to become a leading name in the beverage industry by consistently delivering high-quality, delicious, and healthy drinks. The brand envisions expanding its reach beyond Nagaland, introducing the rich flavors of the region to a broader audience while remaining true to its roots and values. Overall, Nova stands out as a brand that combines local authenticity with modern production techniques to offer superior drinks and juices, making it a trusted choice for consumers seeking refreshing beverage options.
